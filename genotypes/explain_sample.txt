Samples are named as followed:

eg. 2_GRh5 (2 G rh 5)

1: 1/2/3/4

Experiment id, 1 & 2 done with CAS11 soil, 3 & 4 done with CAS12 soil

2: G/N/R/S/C

Genotype, G=Gifu, N=nfr5, R=ram1, S= symrk, C=ccamk

3: R/Rh

Compartment, R=Root or Rh=Rhizosphere. Soil samplesare named as S (e.g. 3_S2 )

4: 1:8 (or _1 : _8 in case of fungal data)

Replicate id
